Failing grades related to study habits

  • Author's viewpoint:

    1. Poor study habits are one of the main causes of failing grades.
    2. Lack of time management and inconsistent studying lead to unsatisfactory performance.
    3. Changing study habits can improve academic performance and help avoid failing.

  • Sophia's viewpoint:

    1. Agrees that ineffective study habits can cause low grades.
    2. Managing time and following a study schedule can help students perform better.
    3. Being consistent in studying makes learning easier.

  • Darrick's viewpoint:

    1. When doing anything productive, if you do something else in the background, it's easy to get distracted.
    2. Even if you feel confident, you have to recall the lesson to see if you are prepared.
    3. When studying, you have to try to remember the lesson instead of just reading it.
