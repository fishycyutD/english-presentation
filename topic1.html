Problems faced by students in school when it comes to grades

  • Author's viewpoint:

    1. Students face many challenges such as difficult subjects, strict teachers, homework, and exams.
    2. Some students experience serious problems like failing grades, inability to pay tuition, bullying, and violation of school rules.
    3. Failing grades are the most feared problem because they lead to pressure, fear, and negative consequences.

  • Sophia's viewpoint:

    1. Agrees that grades cause pressure because they affect a student's confidence and future.
    2. Students often struggle when lessons are difficult or when they lack proper guidance.
    3. Grades become a problem when students do not know how to handle challenges early.

  • Darrick's viewpoint:

    1. Students may get completely stressed over a number or a letter with a sign.
    2. They might be struggling with tests and lose their motivation.
    3. They could think that grades are all that matter.
